About us

A tiny school with big heart

Hikari Nihongo started as a Discord study group between friends in Toronto and Tokyo. Today we welcome learners from 20+ countries into the same cozy vibe.

We are a team of certified Japanese teachers, translators, and long‑time learners who remember exactly how it feels to open a textbook and want to close it again five minutes later. That is why our lessons are story‑driven, visual, and full of chances to actually speak.

Classes are capped at eight students so everyone gets time on the mic. Most of our teachers are based in Japan, with a few night‑owl instructors in North America to cover more time zones.
